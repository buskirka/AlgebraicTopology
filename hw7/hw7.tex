\section{Associativity of Fundamental Group Operator}
\begin{theorem}
The fundamental group operator $\cdot$ is associative; that is,
\[ 
[f] \cdot ([g] \cdot [h]) 
= 
([f] \cdot [g]) \cdot [h]
\]
\end{theorem}
